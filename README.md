##Content Summary##

#####Front#####
Courteous Reader;THe Authors design was to reduce the whole of Mans Being, Business, and Blessedness
#####Body#####

1. First, Of Preparation to Christianity.

1. 2. Of Carriage under the first workings of Christianity.

1. 3: Of the Exercise of spiritual Life.

1. 4. Of your Conversation respecting both others and your self.

1. 5. In watching your heart in natural, civil, and religious actions.

    _ First in General.

    _ The second Natural Action is Rest or Recreation.

    _ The third natural Action is feeding your belly

    _ The fourth Natural action is clothing your body.

    _ The second sort of Actions are Civil, as your Commerce and Trading with others.
